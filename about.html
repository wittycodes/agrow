About Us

Universal Bio-Gas

Universal Biogas is the most advanced, effecient and user friendly household Biogas system in the market.

Universal Biogas fits perfectly in any backyard, converting household food scraps and animal manure into clean cooking gas and liquid fertilizer daily.

Portable Biogas Plants or digesters not just fulfil the purpose of managing a family's waste hygienically and safely, but can also address thier energy requirement. In the newest generation of households biogas systems, waste can be cinverted into energy in hours!

Our Mission

A total commitment to provide Quality Products and ethical services to our customers and partners at all times.

Our Vision

To work together to be the best waste management company and Bio energy producer in India.

Benefits Of Portable Universal Biogas Plants

  • Provides a non-polluting and renewable source of energy.
  • Household wastes and bio-wastes can be disposed of usefully and in a healthy manner.
  • The technology is cheaper and much simpler than those for other bio- fuels , and it is ideal for samll scale application.
  • Provides a source for decentalized power generation.
  • Leads to employment generation in the rural areas.
  • Any biodegradable matter can be used as substrate.
